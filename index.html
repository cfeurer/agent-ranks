Policies & Agreements

Scoring Policy (summary)

We score agents using 50+ signals: neighborhood specialization, property type fit, price range overlap, buyer/seller focus, resources (marketing & negotiation), brokerage marketing power, and success & track record (seller: DOM & SP/LP vs area; buyer: price improvement vs area). Scores are normalized; responsiveness and reliability can adjust the total. We do not accept pay-to-rank. If a top match is outside our eligible pool (no referral agreement yet), we will attempt to onboard them within hours; otherwise we present the next closest eligible match.

Privacy (summary)

We collect minimal data (email/mobile) to register you as a Protected Lead (PLID). We do not reveal your identity or contact information to any agent until you tap Connect and the agent accepts our terms. We store timestamps and limited metadata to protect introductions and to improve the service. We do not sell personal data.

Affiliation Disclosure

Agent Ranks is operated independently and scores agents without brokerage preference. Ownership and management are structured to avoid conflicts; no brokerage receives favorable weighting. An external advisor may review our scoring changes periodically.

Program Participation Agreement (draft) 
PROGRAM PARTICIPATION AGREEMENT (DRAFT SUMMARY)
1. Fee. Agent agrees to pay Agent Ranks (“AR”) a referral fee equal to 25% of agent-side GCI for each Protected Lead that closes.
2. Protected Lead. A client introduced by AR (PLID) for 24 months from first introduction, covering any property within the stated markets.
3. Direction to Pay. Agent instructs title/closing attorney to disburse AR’s fee on the settlement statement or by separate invoice.
4. Survival & Portability. Obligations survive brokerage changes; Agent will provide updated Direction to Pay within 3 business days of any move.
5. Notification & Audit. Agent will notify AR upon contract and provide closing contact info; AR may request reasonable documentation to confirm close.
6. Late & Costs. Late amounts accrue interest; reasonable attorneys’ fees and collections costs are recoverable.
7. Non-Circumvention. Agent shall not circumvent AR’s role; agreed remedies apply (to be finalized by counsel).
8. Data & Privacy. Minimal data used for the introduction and administration; confidentiality applies.
